Avantaro Technologies Pvt Ltd

Account health, settlements, and compliance — simplified for Amazon sellers.

What we do

Avantaro helps Amazon sellers and their advisors keep accounts healthy and compliant. We connect to Amazon’s Selling Partner API (SP-API) to:

  • Monitor key Account Health and performance metrics.
  • Reconcile Amazon settlement reports with GST obligations, including TCS.
  • Surface issues early so sellers can fix them before they become suspensions, penalties, or cash-flow problems.

Our goal is simple: keep your Amazon business in laminar flow by making it easier to follow Amazon policies and Indian tax rules.

Who we serve

  • Amazon sellers who want clear visibility into account health and settlements.
  • Chartered accountants and advisors who manage multiple seller accounts and need less manual spreadsheet work.

Data handling

  • We access Amazon seller data only when a seller or their authorised advisor connects an account via Amazon’s official SP-API authorisation flow.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in secure cloud infrastructure hosted in AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai).
  • Sensitive credentials and tokens are encrypted at rest and access is restricted to authorised personnel.
