Data Protection & Privacy Policy

Avantaro Technologies Private Limited | FinFlow Nexus

Version 1.2 | Effective: April 2026

1. Data Collection & Purpose

FinFlow Nexus collects data from the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) solely for Account Health Monitoring, Policy Compliance Automation, and Suspension Risk Prediction. We operate on a "Pre-Crime" model to protect sellers. We do not sell data to third parties.

2. Encryption (Amazon Compliance)

All Amazon SP-API authentication tokens (LWA Refresh Tokens) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. All data in transit between our infrastructure and Amazon is secured via TLS 1.3.

3. Data Residency (DPDP Act 2023)

In strict compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, all infrastructure, computation, and databases are hosted physically within India via the AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai) region.

4. Retention & Deletion

Upon account disconnection or service termination, all associated SP-API tokens and Personally Identifiable Information (PII) are permanently purged from our databases within 24 hours. API logs are purged every 90 days.
