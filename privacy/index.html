Privacy Policy

Avantaro Technologies Pvt Ltd

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who we are

Avantaro Technologies Pvt Ltd (“Avantaro”, “we”, “our”, “us”) builds tools that help Amazon sellers and their advisors monitor account health, reconcile settlements, and manage compliance with marketplace and tax regulations.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an Avantaro account, we collect basic details such as your name, email address, and organisation information.
  • Amazon seller data. When you connect an Amazon seller account to Avantaro using Amazon’s official Selling Partner API (SP-API) authorisation flow, we access seller data such as orders, settlements, and account-health related metrics that are necessary to provide our services. We follow a data-minimisation approach and do not request data we do not need.

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide dashboards that show account-health and operational metrics.
  • Reconcile Amazon settlement reports with GST obligations, including TCS.
  • Send proactive notifications when important metrics move into risk zones, so you can take action early.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our services, and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data sharing

We may share data only with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers that host our application and databases.
  • Payment service providers that process subscription payments.
  • Regulated financial partners, but only when you explicitly apply for financial products and only to the extent required to evaluate and provide those products.

We may also share information where required by law or valid legal process.

5. Data security and residency

  • All communication between our systems, your browser, and Amazon’s SP-API is protected using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Sensitive credentials and tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel and logged.
  • Our primary infrastructure and databases are hosted in AWS’s ap-south-1 (Mumbai) region to align with Indian data-protection requirements.

6. Retention and deletion

  • We retain your data for as long as your account remains active and for a limited period afterwards, as needed for legal, accounting, or operational purposes.
  • When you disconnect an Amazon account or close your Avantaro account, we delete associated SP-API tokens and personal information within a reasonable timeframe.
  • We may retain aggregated and anonymised data that no longer identifies you for analytics and service improvement.

7. Your rights and contact

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support@avantaro.in
